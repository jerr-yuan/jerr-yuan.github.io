Background

About

Risk strategist by training, agent architect by direction.

EN

I lead AI strategy architecture for post-loan operations — covering customer profiling, collection touch design, and the systems beneath them. Most of my work sits at the intersection of business risk intuition and agent system design: defining objective functions, drawing guardrail boundaries, building evaluation frameworks, and red-teaming what an autonomous agent should and shouldn't do.

Before that, I spent seven years building credit-limit, term, pricing, and collection strategies in consumer lending — the kind of rule-driven precision work that the agent paradigm is now reshaping. That gives me a rare seat: I've actually written the rules I'm now arguing should be replaced.

I'm currently exploring opportunities at AI-native firms and tier-one fintech where agent systems meet real credit & collection workflows.

中文

我现在主导贷后场景的 AI 策略架构——客户画像、触达设计、以及承接这些策略的系统层。我的工作位于业务风险直觉与 Agent 系统设计的交叉点：定义目标函数、画出 Guardrail 边界、搭建评估框架、用红队思维拷问"Agent 应该做什么、不应该做什么"。
